How we're driving change

We are uniting Colorado's startup community with organizations working to lower the amount of death and disability due to mental illness in the state.

We work with companies to create safer, healthier workplaces with clear channels to help for employees who need it.

We use Design Thinking Workshops to unify the experience of our mental health community with the innovation of our startup community.

Together we are building a larger, more effective, empowered mental health community.
